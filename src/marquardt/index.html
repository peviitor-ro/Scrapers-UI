About us

With more than 10,600 employees working in 20 locations in 13 countries on 4 continents, Marquardt Group is a global leader in innovative mechatronics solutions. Our products are used worldwide in the fields of electronic and electromechanical switching systems - not only for the automotive industry, but also for electric power-tools or household appliances. Marquardt also has notable successes in the field of intelligent battery systems for electric cars. Since 2006, the year when the Sibiu branch started to operate, Marquardt Romania carries out its development and design activity in the Research and Development Center, while the production is ensured in 5 large departments: Electronics, Microswitches, Plastic Molding, Painting & Laser and Assembly. The newest unit of the subsidiary is the Marquardt Business Services, a service center opened in 2020. Marquardt Sibiu owes its continuous development on the sustained work and talent of 2,500 employees. After 15 years of activity in Romania, we continue to be passionately committed to our mission to design, develop and deliver quality products for the most famous companies around the world.
